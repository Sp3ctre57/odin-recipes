Fried Rice Recipe:

This is a simple "5 minute"
(Yeah we all know its not five minutes) recipe for
delicious fried rice.
Try it and thank me later

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 ½ cups quick-cooking brown rice
  • 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  • small yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 small green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 cup frozen petite peas
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • ¼ cup roasted peanuts (Optional)

Steps

  • Step 1

    • Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.
    Stir in rice. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until liquid is absorbed,
    about 20 minutes. Set aside.

  • Step 2

    • Heat peanut oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
    Add onions, bell pepper, garlic, and red pepper flakes.
    Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes.

  • Step 3

    • Increase heat to medium-high.
    Stir in cooked rice, green onions, and soy sauce;
    cook and stir for 1 minute.

  • Step 4

    • Add peas and cook for 1 minute more.

  • Step 5

    • Remove from heat. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with peanuts.
